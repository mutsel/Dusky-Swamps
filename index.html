Dusky Swamps

About the Game

Welcome to my first game! It is a small 2D plattformer, where you play as a lost alien on it's way back home. Your goal is to find enough gems to be able to repair your spaceship. But watch out! Dangerous creatures are coming for your and your precious gems. Fortunately you are able to defend yourself with your magic attack!
