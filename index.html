Dusky Swamps

Welcome to my first game! It is a small 2D plattformer, where you play as a lost alien on it's way back home. Your goal is to find enough gems to be able to repair your spaceship.
But watch out! Dangerous creatures are coming for your and your precious gems.
Fortunately you are able to defend yourself with your magic attack!

Gems are precious and you
should try to collect them all
Magic stones are needed
to fill up your magic attacks
Cacti hurt you when
jumping on top of them
Frogs can attack you
with their tongue
The Endboss has more energy.
Moreover he can shoot canonballs
